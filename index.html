Pine

Basic Data

Pine is a softwood coniferous tree.

Latin name:
Pinus
Division:
Pinophyta
Class:
Pinopsida

Where You Can Find Pines

Pines grow in

  1. Europe and
  2. Asia.

My Opinion

I like pines, because

  • they have long needles,
  • smell nice and
  • thanks to the softwood they are easy to cut.

Oak

Oak is a deciduous tree with hardwood. It lives long and grows slowly. Its leaves are simple, lobate and fall off before the winter. It belongs to the genus Quercus.
